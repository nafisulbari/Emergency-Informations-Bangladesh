Emergency Informations Bangladesh

An open database of emergency information accessible by the people of Bangladesh. This project has been initiated to support the lost, injured people to get quick help. Also, create a digital central database of information for the Government, Hospital, and Police.

How It Works

Citizens can view only emergency information upon search or Qr code scanning. Only hospitals can see your medical records and Police stations can view criminal records.
